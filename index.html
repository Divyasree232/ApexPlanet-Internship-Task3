💡 Data-Driven Business Insights

Operations

Null Weekend Premium (0.0% Lift)

A major finding is that Weekend AOV is identical to Weekday AOV ($8.66). The t-test confirms no statistical difference (p=0.995). Rather than expecting higher-spend behaviors on weekends, focus staffing and operational resources to handle raw transaction traffic peaks rather than premium menus.

Marketing

Leverage Food Revenue Drivers

Food items generate 50.0% of total revenue ($43.3K) and have the highest average ticket sizes ($9.58-$9.65). Run targeted bundling strategies (e.g., Beverage + Food combos) to increase overall average order values since beverages on their own exhibit lower tickets (~$7.70).

Inventory

Monthly Seasonal Planning

Revenue peaked in June ($7,136) and October ($7,073), with major drops in February ($6,455, down 8.1%). Align raw ingredient inventory levels with historical seasonal patterns to optimize cash flow and reduce waste during low-demand months.
