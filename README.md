

## Business Analytics Dashboard Project

### Project Overview

This project aims to solve complex, multi-faceted business problems through in-depth data analysis and the development of an interactive business intelligence dashboard. The analysis focuses on identifying key performance metrics, exploring a critical business area in detail, and presenting insights through a user-friendly dashboard for decision-making.

---

## Objectives

1. Define and track core business KPIs.
2. Perform a deep-dive analysis on a selected business area.
3. Build an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders.
4. Generate actionable insights and recommendations.

---

## Project Workflow

### Step 1: Define Core KPIs

The following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are identified to measure business performance:

#### 1. Conversion Rate

**Formula:**

```
Conversion Rate = (Number of Conversions / Total Visitors) × 100
```

**Business Rationale:**
Measures how effectively visitors are converted into customers.

---

#### 2. Customer Churn Rate

**Formula:**

```
Churn Rate = (Customers Lost During Period / Total Customers at Start of Period) × 100
```

**Business Rationale:**
Tracks customer retention and identifies customer loss trends.

---

#### 3. Average Order Value (AOV)

**Formula:**

```
AOV = Total Revenue / Total Number of Orders
```

**Business Rationale:**
Helps evaluate customer purchasing behavior and revenue generation.

---

#### 4. Customer Retention Rate

**Formula:**

```
Retention Rate = ((Customers at End - New Customers) / Customers at Start) × 100
```

**Business Rationale:**
Measures the company's ability to retain existing customers.

---

#### 5. Revenue Growth Rate

**Formula:**

```
Revenue Growth Rate = ((Current Revenue - Previous Revenue) / Previous Revenue) × 100
```

**Business Rationale:**
Indicates overall business growth and financial performance.
